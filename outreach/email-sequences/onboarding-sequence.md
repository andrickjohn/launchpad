# Onboarding Sequence — VA Users
# Trigger: VA accepts invitation
# Steps: 2 emails

## Email 1 — Immediate
Subject: You've been invited to LaunchPad — here's what you need to know
Body: What LaunchPad is. What you can do (execute approved actions).
What you can't do (approve, change templates, access billing).
How the activity log works. Link to get started.

## Email 2 — Day 2
Subject: Quick guide: Executing outreach tasks in LaunchPad
Body: Step-by-step walkthrough of the VA workflow:
1. Check the approval queue for tasks
2. Execute approved actions
3. Log completion
4. Flag any issues
