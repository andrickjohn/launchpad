# Welcome Sequence
# Trigger: New user signs up (self-use) or new VA is invited
# Steps: 3 emails over 5 days

## Email 1 — Immediate
Subject: Welcome to LaunchPad — here's how to get started
Body: Quick start guide. Three things to do first:
1. Create your first campaign
2. Import or add your first prospects
3. Let the AI score them
Include link to the Campaign Setup Wizard.

## Email 2 — Day 2
Subject: Your first campaign playbook is ready
Body: If they created a campaign, reference the AI-generated brief.
If not, encourage them to try the Campaign Wizard.
Show an example of what a generated playbook looks like.

## Email 3 — Day 5
Subject: How's your first week going?
Body: Quick tips based on what they've done (or haven't).
Link to help resources. Encourage Assisted mode if they're on Manual.
